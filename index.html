About

I spent over a decade in insurance and financial consulting — including running my own Allianz agency for five years, serving small and mid-sized businesses across the Ruhr region. That's where I learned how real companies operate: where money leaks, what owners worry about at 2 a.m., and why most "solutions" from outside vendors don't fit how the business actually works.

In 2022 I retrained as a software developer through a coding bootcamp, turning a long-standing fascination with technology into a profession, and have been shipping production software since. Today I focus on AI-assisted automation, and I'm currently the sole technical lead on a major AI integration at a mid-size German consulting firm — covering meeting-to-document automation, knowledge vault architecture, and LLM-powered agent workflows.

What I offer is unusual: a developer who also speaks the language of business operations. I understand cash flow, staffing, client relationships, and the daily friction of running a company — because I've lived on that side of the desk. That means less time spent explaining your business to me, and more time building the solution.

I believe software development without AI assistance is no longer professionally responsible. AI is not a gimmick to bolt on top of existing processes — it's the leverage that lets a one-person engagement deliver what used to require a team.

Based in the Ruhr region. Working remotely with clients across Germany and internationally.
